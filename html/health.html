COVID-19 FULL HEALTH GUIDLINE

Handwashing should be done often

Hands should be washed frequently with soap and water for at least 1 minute, giving attention to all regions of the fingers and hand.

Keep your distance from others

Avoid close contact with sick people or those who have returned from high or medium-risk nations by keeping a distance of at least 2 meters.

Using a tissue, cough or sneeze

When coughing or sneezing, cover your nose and mouth with a tissue. Immediately dispose of the tissue in a safe place.

Quarantine for all travelers is required by the state

All citizens returning to the state are required to spend 14 days in quarantine.

If it is not absolutely necessary, do not leave the house

Stay at home and do not go out

Observe the guidelines

Follow the Ministry of Health's suggestions and be informed about new developments and directions from the appropriate agencies

Be sure to wear face mask

Disinfect regularly

Disinfectants or alcohol tissues can be used.

14 days of compulsory self-isolation

All those who came into contact with sick people or were exposed to COVID-19 must report themselves and stay in their houses for 14 days in self-isolation.
